Inflation

Many have heard on any outlet of media that inflation is on the rise. Taken from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (https://www.bls.gov/cpi/tables/supplemental-files/), this dataset documents the increase of the Consumer Price Index (CPI) all the way from the 1920s to current day. The CPI indicates the price of a weight average market basket of consumer goods and services purchased by househols. This first visualization is to visualize for you how fast the CPI has been increasing as it approaches current day. Despite economic pressures, consumers still spend on leisure. Here, we compare CPI trends with video game sales. How does this spending compare to what someone earns weekly?

Shifting Priorities in Spending

Inflation has not only influenced major prices across the board, but has also influenced how we allocate money on non-essentials as well! Using this dataset, (https://store.nba.com/jerseys/), the pie chart highlights how spending wages on regular items such as NBA merchandise has evolved during inflationary periods. Examining inflation's impact on retail jersey prices from 2005 to 2025, the pie chart illustrates the toll inflation has taken on Sports merchandise and it's average price.
